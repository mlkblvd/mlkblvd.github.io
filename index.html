Our Goals

  • Build strong communities

  • Increase poverty awareness

  • Reduce homelessness

  • Create jobs

  • Fight hunger and food insecurity


    • The Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard Foundation connects, serves, and empowers citizens in need by providing free programs and services that address their needs and fill gaps in government care.


    Our Mission Statement

    The Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard Foundation invisions a world where the streets that bear the great name of Martin Luther King Jr. are a beacon of hope for other communities to follow.

    Our charity emulates the values of Martin Luther King Jr. like equality, liberty, nonviolence, education, love, selflessness, leadership, and hope in all that we do.

    We strive for a better world. One that includes all members of society no matter how strong or weak, rich or poor, or like Dr. King once said by the color of their skin or content of their character.

    Donations to our charity will be allocated responsibly and appropriately to impact communities as beneficially as possible.
